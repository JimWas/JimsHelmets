The EPS Advantage: 98% Air

Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is not just "styrofoam." It is a high-density, energy-absorbing liner. When an impact occurs, the microscopic cells collapse, absorbing the energy that would otherwise fracture a child's thinner skull.

The Tropical Chimney Design

A major reason parents avoid helmets is the fear of their child "overheating" in 35°C humidity. Our Jim's Helmet features **Passive Internal Ducting**—channels molded into the EPS that force hot air out the back while the motorcycle is in motion.

Front Intake

Positioned at the brow to catch the wind at speeds as low as 10km/h.

Internal Channels

Grooves in the EPS liner create space between the head and the shell, preventing heat traps.

Rear Exhaust

Leverages the "Venturi Effect" to pull hot air away from the scalp as the bike moves.
