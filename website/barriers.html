Behavioral & Cognitive Barriers

Low compliance is driven by deeply rooted misconceptions and environmental factors:

  • Short-Trip Fallacy: Misconception that low speeds pose no risk.
  • Somatic Myths: Belief that helmet weight harms skull/neck development.
  • Heat/Discomfort: Heavy, non-ventilated helmets trap tropical heat.
  • Economic Constraints: Prohibitive cost of certified gear.
