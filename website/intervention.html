School-Based Intervention Pathway

We deploy a structured 12-week program designed to build a localized culture of safety.

  1. Audit: Target schools with high motorcycle commute rates.
  2. Covenant: Parent-Teacher agreement for mandatory helmet use.
  3. Handover: High-visibility public ceremonies.
  4. Integration: Extracurricular road safety curriculum.
  5. Sustainability: Trade-in program for outgrown helmets.
