Governance & Transparency

We operate as a dual-entity non-profit to ensure fiscal responsibility and legal compliance in both the US and Cambodia.

  • US Entity: 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status, managing capital and providing governance oversight.
  • Cambodian NGO: Registered entity executing field delivery, compliant with LANGO regulations.
  • Accountability: Meticulous adherence to GDT, ACAR, and MoLVT regulations, with annual independent audits.
