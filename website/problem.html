Pediatric Road Safety in Cambodia

Road traffic injuries are the leading cause of death and permanent disability among children and adolescents in Cambodia. At least four children lose their lives on Cambodia's roads every day.

Why is this happening?

  • Rapid motorization without adequate safety infrastructure.
  • Low helmet compliance among child passengers.
  • Cognitive barriers like the "Short-Trip Fallacy."
  • Somatic myths regarding helmet safety.
