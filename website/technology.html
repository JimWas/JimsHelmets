Engineering Safety: The Tropical Helmet

Standard adult helmets are physically incompatible with children. We engineer solutions specific to the Cambodian context.

  • Impact Absorption: EPS liner reduces peak impact forces from 450 Gs (in plastic caps) to ~150 Gs.
  • Thermal Engineering: Passive ducting and Venturi-effect exhaust ports maintain comfort in 35°C heat.
  • Ergonomics: Ultra-lightweight 280g profile eliminates neck strain for toddlers and young children.
  • Certification: Meets international standards (ECE 22.06).
