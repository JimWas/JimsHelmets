# Jim's Helmets

Jim's Helmets is a non-profit initiative dedicated to addressing the pediatric road safety crisis in Cambodia. We focus on providing safety-certified, tropically ventilated helmets and fostering a culture of safety through evidence-based school intervention programs.

## Mission
To reduce pediatric road traffic fatalities and permanent brain injuries in Cambodia by eliminating behavioral and physiological barriers to consistent helmet use among children.

## The Problem
Road traffic injuries are the leading cause of death and permanent disability among children in Cambodia, with at least four children losing their lives on the roads every day. This crisis is fueled by rapid motorization, inadequate safety infrastructure, and persistent cognitive and socioeconomic barriers to consistent helmet use.

## The Solution: Tropical Helmets
We provide tropically ventilated, high-quality safety helmets that are:
- **Lightweight (280g):** Anatomically compatible with children.
- **Ventilated:** Engineered to prevent thermal buildup in Cambodia's climate.
- **Certified:** Meeting international safety standards (ECE 22.06).

## Our Approach
We implement a structured, 12-week school-based intervention pathway modeled on successful regional frameworks:
1. School Auditing & Training
2. Parent-Teacher Safety Covenant
3. Sovereign Handover Ceremonies
4. Curriculum Integration
